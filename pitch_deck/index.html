The Problem: India's Radiology Crisis

Radiologist Shortage

Severe shortage in tier-2, tier-3 cities resulting in massive backlogs.

Delayed Diagnosis

Average 24–48 hour turnaround time for critical scans.

Emergency Bottleneck

Critical cases delayed leading to increased mortality risks.

Rural Access Gap

Lack of expert interpretation in remote village healthcare centers.

India has ~20,000 radiologists for 1.4 billion people
